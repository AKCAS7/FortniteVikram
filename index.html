Why I love the Game

I love this game due to its mix of building and shooting.People hold mixed opinions about this but I dont really care.

most amazing feelings I get.
It feels very deserving to beat the opponent after building, whereas in other shooting games when you just stand there and
shoot the other guy, I am doing the SAME THING EVERY guy in that game does.
SO thats why I like this game so much.
